Items and Weapons
	weapondamage
		is a % that when multiplied by player's base damage
		it adds to that, making the player stronger
		
	weaponeffect
		if a special weapon (*), can have effects, like
		lighting a target on fire, causing paralysis, or
		others.

	armorprotection
		Same concept as weapon damage, % multiplied by base
		health and that value added to health to give extra
		protection

	armoreffect
		Same as weaponeffect, only giving immunities to the
		player
